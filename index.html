About Me

Full Stack Web Developer by day and Film Composer by night– Joe graduated with a Master’s Degree in film scoring from Berklee College of Music in Valencia, Spain and is also a graduate of the University of Texas at Austin with a certificate in Full Stack Web Development.

Currently in Austin, Texas. Joe has written music for a variety of styles and projects for feature length films, commercials, video games, promo videos, and concerts among a number of other things. Having a passion for writing strong and memorable themes for character and story development, he constantly strives to move audiences by telling a story in conjunction with the film on screen. Recent credits include commercials for 3M and VolunteerSpot, informational videos for the Texas Public Policy Foundation, and several short films for Robinson Pictures.
